# What is GEO? (Generative Engine Optimization)
Generative Engine Optimization (GEO) is the practice of optimizing content for AI-powered engines that generate synthesized overviews from multiple sources. In 2026, Google’s AI Overviews and Perplexity drive over 40% of non-branded search traffic (BrightEdge, 2026). GEO requires content to be structured, well-cited, and easily extractable by AI systems.
